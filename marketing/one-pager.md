## THE PROBLEM

- Businesses spend $500-$5,000/month on AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Jasper)
- **Privacy concerns** prevent use with sensitive data (contracts, patient records)
- **Poor integration** between tools (manual copy/paste)
- **High costs** ($20-$60/user/month add up fast)
- **No customization** for specific workflows

**40% of businesses cite privacy as top AI adoption barrier.** (Gartner 2025)

**Key Benefits:**
- ✅ **80% cost savings** vs. cloud AI over 3 years

- ✅ **Zero IT expertise** required (plug & play)

- ✅ **No vendor lock-in** (open-source foundation)
